Manual Override

Bypassing the API directly links your browser to the video stream feed.
How to find direct stream URLs?

1. Open 111movies.net in a browser tab and load the movie/show.

2. Right-click anywhere and select Inspect (or press F12) to open Developer Tools.

3. Go to the Network tab and filter by m3u8 or mp4.

4. Play the video, look for requests starting with master.m3u8 or containing .mp4, copy the link, and paste it here.
